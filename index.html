How to measure

To find the perfect size of your bra you need two values: the bust line and the band line

Bust

To find the circumference of the breast, keep your back straight, upright, with your arms soft at your sides; then pass the tape measure to the height of the nipples

Band

Stay in the same position, and pass the tape measure under the breast
